A couple things I enjoy in life

Carshows

Going to carshows and seeing people express themselves through their vehicles is always fun. I'm a huge car geek. I hope I never get tired of apreciating the curves and lines of automobiles and other art on wheels. vehicles will always be visually and audibly stimulating to me.

Riding motorcycles

There is nothing like riding a motorcycle. Everytime you get to your destination safely you really have a new appriciation for life. Dollar for dollar its hard to beat the acceleration of a motorcycle. The feeling of getting out on a curvy road with no destination is one of the best types of therapy.

Exercise

Besides learning, I beleive exercise is one of the best ways to invest in yourself. Not only do you build confidencein yourself, you are also more of a help to others around you. Exercise can also and years to your life.
